Chole bhature

Price: 50 /-

Description: Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) paired with deep-fried, fluffy bread (bhature). The chickpeas are cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy made with onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, and dried mango powder, which gives it a tangy flavor. Bhature is made from a fermented dough of all-purpose flour, yogurt, and a leavening agent, rolled out and fried until golden and puffed. Often served with pickles, onions, and a side of yogurt, this hearty and flavorful combination is a favorite for breakfast, lunch, or special occasions across India.
