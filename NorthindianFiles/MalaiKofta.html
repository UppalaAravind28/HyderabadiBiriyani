Malai Kofta

Price: 45 /-

Description: Malai Kofta is a rich and indulgent North Indian curry dish made with soft, melt-in-the-mouth koftas (dumplings) served in a creamy, flavorful gravy. The koftas are typically prepared from a mixture of mashed potatoes, paneer (Indian cottage cheese), and spices, often stuffed with nuts and raisins for added texture and flavor, then deep-fried until golden. The gravy is a luxurious blend of tomatoes, onions, cashew paste, cream, and aromatic spices like cardamom, cumin, and garam masala, resulting in a smooth and mildly spiced sauce. Malai Kofta is often served with naan, roti, or basmati rice, making it a popular choice for festive meals and special occasions.
