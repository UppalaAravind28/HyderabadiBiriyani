Palak paneer

Price: 50 /-

Description: Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian dish made with tender cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a smooth and flavorful spinach-based gravy. The dish is prepared by blanching fresh spinach (palak) and blending it into a vibrant green puree, which is then cooked with onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a medley of spices such as cumin, garam masala, and turmeric. Cream or yogurt is often added to enhance the richness and balance the flavors. Palak Paneer is a nutritious and wholesome dish, packed with the goodness of spinach and protein from paneer, and is best enjoyed with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
