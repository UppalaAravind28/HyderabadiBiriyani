Vada

Price: 50 /-

Description: Vada is a crispy and savory South Indian snack made from ground lentils, typically urad dal (black gram). The soaked lentils are ground into a smooth batter and mixed with spices such as chopped green chilies, curry leaves, ginger, and sometimes onions or black pepper for added flavor. The batter is shaped into doughnut-like rings and deep-fried until golden and crispy on the outside while remaining soft on the inside. Vada is a versatile dish often served with chutneys, sambar, or enjoyed as part of a traditional South Indian breakfast or snack platter.
