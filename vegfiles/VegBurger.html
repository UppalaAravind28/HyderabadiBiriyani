Veg Burger

Description: A veg burger is a delicious, plant-based delight that combines wholesome ingredients in a soft, toasted bun. At its heart is a patty made from vegetables, lentils, beans, or a blend of grains, seasoned with spices for a flavorful bite. It’s typically layered with fresh lettuce, juicy tomato slices, crunchy onions, and a tangy sauce like mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard. Often garnished with a slice of cheese or pickles, the burger balances textures and flavors perfectly. Popular as a healthier alternative to meat burgers, it’s a versatile and satisfying option for vegetarians and food lovers alike.
