Description: A veg pizza is a vibrant and flavorful delight featuring a crispy or soft crust as its base, topped with a rich tomato sauce and a generous layer of melted cheese. Perfect for vegetarians and pizza lovers alike, it balances freshness and indulgence, making it a versatile favorite for any occasion.
