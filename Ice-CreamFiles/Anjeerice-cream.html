Anjeer ice-cream

Price: 89 /-

Description:Anjeer (fig) ice cream is a luxurious dessert that combines the natural sweetness and chewy texture of figs with the creamy indulgence of ice cream. Made by blending fig puree or chunks of dried anjeer into a rich, velvety base, this treat delivers a distinctive flavor that is both earthy and mildly nutty. The figs, often soaked beforehand, infuse the ice cream with their unique taste and occasionally add delightful bursts of texture. Perfect as a standalone dessert or paired with nuts and honey, anjeer ice cream is a sophisticated choice that offers a wholesome twist to traditional frozen treats.
