Ambur biryani

Price: 399 /-

Description: Ambur biryani is a flavorful and iconic dish from the town of Ambur in Tamil Nadu, renowned for its unique taste and simplicity. This biryani is made with fragrant seeraga samba rice, small-grained and perfect for absorbing the rich flavors of the dish. It is traditionally prepared with marinated chicken or mutton, cooked with a blend of spices, dried red chilies, fresh coriander, mint leaves, and yogurt, giving it a mildly spiced yet deeply aromatic profile. Unlike heavily layered biryanis, Ambur biryani is cooked in a one-pot style, ensuring every grain of rice is infused with the meat’s flavor. Often served with a side of brinjal curry (ennai kathirikai) or onion raita, it is a beloved staple of Tamil Nadu’s culinary tradition.
