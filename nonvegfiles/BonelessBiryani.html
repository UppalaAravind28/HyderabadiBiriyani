Boneless Biriyani

Description:Boneless Biryani is a luxurious and convenient version of the classic biryani, featuring tender, boneless pieces of marinated meat or chicken cooked to perfection with fragrant basmati rice. The dish is layered with rich, spiced gravy made from caramelized onions, tomatoes, yogurt, and a medley of aromatic spices such as cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. The boneless meat ensures every bite is flavorful and hassle-free, making it an ideal choice for those who prefer an effortless dining experience. Garnished with fried onions, fresh mint, and coriander, and often infused with saffron milk or ghee, Boneless Biryani is a harmonious blend of taste, aroma, and indulgence.
