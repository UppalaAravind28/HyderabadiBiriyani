LollipopBiriyani

Price: 349/-

Description:Lollipop Biryani is a fusion dish that creatively combines the rich, aromatic flavors of traditional biryani with the bold and crispy allure of chicken lollipops. This unique preparation starts with fragrant basmati rice cooked to perfection, layered with a blend of caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a spiced gravy infused with the warmth of biryani masala. Juicy, marinated chicken lollipops, fried to golden crispness, are placed atop the biryani, adding a flavorful and textural contrast. Garnished with fried onions, mint leaves, and a drizzle of saffron milk, Lollipop Biryani is a feast for the senses, perfect for food enthusiasts who love experimenting with bold and indulgent flavors.
