Mutton Biriyani

Price: 499/-

Description:Mutton Biryani is a rich and aromatic dish that combines tender pieces of marinated mutton with fragrant basmati rice, cooked together to create a harmonious blend of flavors. The mutton is slow-cooked with a medley of spices such as cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves, and cardamom, along with caramelized onions, tomatoes, and yogurt to form a robust and flavorful gravy. The partially cooked rice is layered over the mutton, often infused with saffron milk, and steamed to perfection in the traditional dum style, sealing in all the aromas. Garnished with fried onions, fresh mint, and coriander, Mutton Biryani is a hearty and indulgent meal, celebrated for its melt-in-your-mouth mutton and rich, spicy undertones.
