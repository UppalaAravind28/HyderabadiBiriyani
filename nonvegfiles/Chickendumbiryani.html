Chicken dum biryani

Price: 349 /-

Description: Chicken dum biryani is a popular and aromatic rice dish celebrated for its rich flavors and traditional cooking method. It is prepared by layering marinated chicken with partially cooked basmati rice, fragrant spices, caramelized onions, and fresh herbs like mint and coriander. The dish is then slow-cooked using the dum method, where the pot is sealed to trap steam, allowing the flavors to meld together and the chicken to become tender and juicy. Saffron-infused milk and ghee are often added for a luxurious aroma and taste. Typically served with raita, spicy salan, or pickles, chicken dum biryani is a hearty and satisfying meal cherished across India and beyond.
