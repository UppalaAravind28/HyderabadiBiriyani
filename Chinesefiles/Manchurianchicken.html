Manchurian chicken

Price: 120 /-

Description: Chicken Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that features crispy fried chicken pieces coated in a flavorful, tangy, and slightly spicy sauce. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and other seasonings, then battered and deep-fried until golden and crispy. The sauce is made with ingredients like soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and sometimes ketchup, along with vegetables such as onions, bell peppers, and garlic, creating a savory, sweet, and spicy combination. The dish is often garnished with spring onions and served as an appetizer or with fried rice or noodles, making it a favorite in many Indian and Asian fusion restaurants.
